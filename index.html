What type of writing or project qualifies?

Anything that focuses on producing long-format writing content that can be traditionally or digitally published. It can be comics, novels, historical or literary fiction and everything else in-between.

Wait, is this a grant or an investment?

This is an investment, not a grant. In exchange for the $10,000 funding, we offer writers the choice of either:

  • 15% revenue share from all future book sales and related income, or
  • 5% equity stake in the writer's publishing business or intellectual property rights

This constitutes a securities offering. Investment terms are subject to final legal documentation.

How will you select the winners?

We're looking for smart and energetic people with fun and interesting ideas. Drafts are a big plus; not just because they're great to read through, but because they hint at the type of person that cares about the reader's experience, which is ultimately all that matters.

Can I apply if I've raised money already for my book through crowdfunding?

Yes.

Who is funding this?

Christos Alexiou has committed over $500,000 to Writers Fund. All investments are made through Writers Fund Inc, a legally established investment entity in Delaware, US.
