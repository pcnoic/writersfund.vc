Writers Fund — accelerator for writers

  • $10,000 paid out in 12-month increments to fund a writer with modest living for 1 year
  • Legal and publication expenses are paid out from the fund when the writer decides to publish.
  • Created by Christos Alexiou
  • Applications are always open

What type of writing or project qualifies?

Anything that focuses on producing long format writing content that can be traditionally or digitally published. It can be comics, novels, historical or literary fiction and everything else in-between.

Wait, is this a grant or an investment?

It is an investment. We offer to the writer the choice of either a 15% royalty share or a 5% equity stake.

How will you select the winners?

We're looking for smart and energetic people with actionable ideas that are clearly useful. Drafts are big plus; not just because they're great to read through, but because they hint at the type of person that cares about the reader's experience, which is ultimately all that matters.

Can I apply if I've raised money already for my book through crowdfunding?

Yes.
